Prologue

From 1990-Summer 2019

I come from a large Catholic family in Alabama. For as far back as I could remember, I had wanted to be a priest, and spent most of my adult life in that pursuit. It was a wonderful, but trying experience. So trying, that it led me to the difficult conclusion that I could not do it well for the rest of my life, and so it was not my true calling. I knew I needed to make a change.
